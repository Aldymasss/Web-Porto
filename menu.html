Our Concept

Paperplane Project is a cozy cafe combining rustic and contemporary design elements, offering a unique "third place" between home and work. Our warm, minimalist atmosphere features wooden furniture, lush greenery, and ambient lighting, creating an ideal setting for relaxation, casual meetings, or quiet work sessions.

We pride ourselves on thoughtfully crafted food and beverages using quality ingredients, with an emphasis on both tradition and innovation. Every dish and drink tells a story, inviting you to become part of our journey.
